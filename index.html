— 01 Our Story

A pause worth taking.

Tree Coffee Shops began with a simple belief — that a great drink isn't a transaction, it's a small piece of someone's day. So we built a space that respects that.

From the morning espresso to the late-night mojito, every glass that crosses our counter is built with intention. Real fruit. Slow brews. Layered colors that arrive looking the way they taste.

You'll find us in Tripoli, beside Makhazen Condor in Azmi — under green leaves, warm light, and the quiet hum of a café that knows you.

Fresh

Real fruit. Real ice. Real pours. No shortcuts — ever.

Craft

Every drink built by hand. Every glass dressed with care.
